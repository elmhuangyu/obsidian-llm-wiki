# Wiki Index

Welcome to the persistent wiki, a structured and interlinked collection of knowledge.

## Patterns

- [[LLM-Wiki]]: A general pattern for building persistent, compounding knowledge bases using LLMs.
- [[Personal-Knowledge-Wiki]]: A specific agentic implementation of the [[LLM-Wiki]] pattern with defined operational commands and maintenance principles.

## People

- [[Andrej-Karpathy]]: AI researcher and author of the [[LLM-Wiki]] pattern.
- [[Farza]]: Engineer and creator of the Wiki Skill for [[Claude-Code]].
- [[Vitalik-Buterin]]: Co-founder of Ethereum and advocate for [[Self-Sovereign-AI]].

## Tools

- [[Obsidian]]: Markdown-based PKM application used as the "IDE" for the wiki.
- [[Claude-Code]]: Command-line agentic environment for AI-assisted development and knowledge management.
- [[MarkItDown]]: Microsoft tool for converting diverse data into Markdown for the `ingest` phase.
- [[Gemini]]: Google's multimodal models (e.g., Gemini Flash) used for complex data extraction and ingestion.
- [[NixOS]]: A declarative and reproducible Linux distribution used for secure AI stacks.
- [[OpenClaw]]: An open-source framework for building agentic AI (with significant security caveats).

## Concepts

- [[Personal-Knowledge-Management]] (PKM): The broad field of managing individual knowledge.
- [[Self-Sovereign-AI]]: A privacy-first approach to local, secure, and autonomous AI.
- [[RAG]]: Retrieval-Augmented Generation, a technique compared and contrasted with [[LLM-Wiki]].
- [[Memex]]: A historical predecessor for the vision of a curated personal knowledge store.
