# Andrej Karpathy

**Andrej Karpathy** is a computer scientist and artificial intelligence researcher. He is known for his work in deep learning, computer vision, and large language models (LLMs). He has held positions at OpenAI and Tesla (as Director of AI).

## Contributions to Personal Knowledge Management

Karpathy has proposed several influential patterns for using LLMs as tools for cognitive augmentation and personal organization:

- **[[LLM-Wiki]] Pattern**: A framework for building a persistent, compounding knowledge base where an LLM agent serves as the primary maintainer of a structured markdown wiki. This was first described in his 2024 [LLM Wiki Gist](https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f).

## Background

- **Education**: PhD from Stanford University (working with Fei-Fei Li).
- **Career**: Founding member of OpenAI; Director of AI at Tesla (Autopilot/FSD).
- **Education/Community**: Known for "Neural Networks: Zero to Hero" and other deep learning educational content.
